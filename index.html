How it works:

  1. Fill in player names (up to 5 at once)
  2. Set bonus scores if needed (optional, defaults to 0)
  3. Click "Check In All Players" to submit
  4. Only filled-in players will be checked in
  5. Go to Recording tab to record their times

Instructions:

  1. Click "Load Waiting Players" to fetch players from the same check-in batch
  2. Maximum 5 players will be loaded (players checked in together)
  3. Click "Start All Players" when everyone is ready (countdown begins)
  4. Click each player's "Stop" button when they finish
  5. After all players finish, click "Push All Scores to Database"
  6. Repeat to record the next batch of players
